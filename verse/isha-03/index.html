Notes

This mantra introduces the concept of ─Бtmahanas - those who 'kill' or 'destroy' the Self not literally, but by obscuring it through ignorance. ┼Ъaс╣Еkara Bhagavadp─Бda explains this is metaphorical: the Self being eternal cannot actually be killed, but its true nature and results (like immortality and bliss) become hidden from the ignorant. The commentary transitions from this negative description to introduce the positive description of the Self's true nature in the next mantra.
