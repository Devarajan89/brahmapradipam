Notes

The final mantra is a prayer to Agni for guidance on the northern path (devay─Бna) that leads to non-return, as opposed to the southern path (pitс╣Ыy─Бna) of rebirth. The seeker, weary of repeated births and deaths, requests the auspicious path knowing Agni is aware of all deeds.
