This crucial mantra systematically negates all three bodies (gross, subtle, causal) to establish the pure nature of the Self. ┼Ъaс╣Еkara Bhagavadp─Бda's commentary explains how different epithets negate different levels of embodiment: avraс╣Зa and asn─Бvira negate the gross body, ak─Бya negates the subtle body, and ┼Ыuddha negates the causal body by removing the taint of ignorance.
