The Upaniṣads

उपनिषद्

"Sitting Near" the Teacher — Profound Teachings on Brahman

The Upaniṣads form the concluding portions of the Vedas (Vedānta) and contain the essence of Vedic wisdom. They explore the nature of ultimate reality (Brahman), the self (Ātman), and the path to liberation (mokṣa).

The Ten Principal Upaniṣads

Īśa Upaniṣad 18 mantras

ईशोपनिषद्

The first and shortest of the principal Upaniṣads, teaching the all-pervading nature of the Lord and the path of karma and jñāna.

Kena Upaniṣad 4 sections

केनोपनिषद्

Explores the nature of Brahman as the power behind the senses and the mind. Named after its opening word "kena" (by whom).

कठोपनिषद्

Muṇḍaka Upaniṣad 3 chapters
